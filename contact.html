Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common queries

What is the response time for security incidents?

Our SOC team provides 24/7 monitoring with an average response time of under 15 minutes for critical security incidents. We prioritize rapid detection and response to minimize potential damage.

Do you offer customized security solutions?

Yes, we understand that every organization has unique security requirements. Our team works closely with you to develop tailored security strategies that align with your business objectives and compliance needs.

What certifications does your team hold?

Our security professionals hold industry-recognized certifications including CISSP, CEH, OSCP, CISA, and more. We continuously invest in training to stay ahead of emerging threats.

How can I get started with NativeDefence?

Simply fill out the contact form above or reach out to us via email. Our team will schedule a consultation to understand your needs and propose a suitable security solution for your organization.
