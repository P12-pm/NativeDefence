Fortify Your Digital Defenses

At NativeDefence, we serve as your unwavering partner in strengthening your digital security. With a well-established history of excellence in Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT), we are dedicated to safeguarding your digital assets.

Vulnerability Assessment

Uncover vulnerabilities in your digital infrastructure proactively. Our comprehensive assessment meticulously identifies weaknesses, empowering you to address and prioritize remediation effectively for enhanced security.

Penetration Testing

Our team of ethical hackers specializes in emulating real-world cyberattacks. Through Penetration Testing, we reveal vulnerabilities that automated scans might overlook, guaranteeing the fortification of your defenses.

VAPT Methodology

1

Scope

Goal: Define boundaries and limitations.

Clearly outline systems, applications, and networks to be tested along with specific goals.

2

Info Gathering

Goal: Collect essential environment info.

Understand architecture, attack surface, and conduct initial reconnaissance.

3

Vuln Detection

Goal: Identify weaknesses & misconfigurations.

Use automated tools and manual testing to discover security issues.

4

Analysis

Goal: Plan the penetration testing.

Evaluate potential risks and prioritize testing efforts based on gathered data.

5

Privilege Escalation

Goal: Gain deeper access.

Attempt to escalate privileges to assess the full extent of potential breaches.

6

Result Analysis

Goal: Analyze impact.

Understand the significance and potential consequences of identified weaknesses.

7

Reporting

Goal: Document findings.

Create a clear, actionable report for stakeholders with recommendations.

8

Cleanup

Goal: Restore environment.

Ensure no unintentional damage and restore changes made during testing.
