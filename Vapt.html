Methodology

8 Steps of VAPT

1. Scope
Define boundaries, limitations, systems, and goals of the assessment.
2. Information Gathering
Collect essential info, understand architecture, and map the attack surface.
3. Vulnerability Detection
Identify weaknesses, misconfigurations using automated tools and manual testing.
4. Analysis & Planning
Analyze gathered info to prioritize risks and plan penetration testing.
5. Privilege Escalation
Attempt to gain deeper access to assess the extent of potential breaches.
6. Result Analysis
Analyze outcomes and understand the potential consequences of weaknesses.
7. Reporting
Document findings and create actionable reports for stakeholders.
8. Cleanup
Restore changes and ensure no unintentional damage remains.
