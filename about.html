Ashok Pai

Ashok Pai is a seasoned IT professional with over 27 years of experience in the industry. He has a strong background in IT governance, risk, and compliance, with expertise in areas such as information security, ITIL process management, and compliance with various standards including ISO 27001, ISO 20000, and PCI DSS. Ashok has worked with numerous clients across various industries, including manufacturing, pharmaceuticals, banking, and telecom, and has conducted over 300 certification assessments for ISO 27001:2005 and ISO 27001:2013.

Throughout his career, Ashok has held various leadership positions, including CEO and founder of Vanaps Consulting Pvt. Ltd., CEO of Xerder Business Solutions, and senior consultant at Tata Consultancy Services. He has also worked with other notable companies such as Aujas Networks Pvt. Ltd., British Standard Management Systems Pvt. Ltd., and Sysman Computers. Ashok's expertise extends to security posture reporting, information security management, and configuration, problem, and change management of IT infrastructure.

Ashok's accomplishments include providing consulting and operations on risk management and compliance management of IT infrastructure, conducting security audits and vulnerability assessments for various organizations, and delivering training sessions on standards such as ISO 20000 and ISO 27001:2013. He holds a Bachelor of Engineering degree from Pune University and has obtained various professional certifications, including ISO 27001:2005 Lead Auditor, ITIL foundation, and SANS Security Essentials.
